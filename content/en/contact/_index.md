**Room 5223, 2nd floor, Building 5**, Omiya Campus, Shibaura Institute of Technology
307 Fukasaku, Minuma-ku, Saitama City, Saitama 337-8570, Japan

## By train

- From **Higashi-Omiya Station** (JR Utsunomiya Line), East Exit: about 5 min by the campus shuttle bus, or a 20-min walk — [shuttle bus timetable](http://bus.shibaura-it.ac.jp/)
- From **Iwatsuki Station** (Tobu Urban Park Line), West Exit: about 20 min by the campus shuttle bus — [timetable (PDF)](https://www.shibaura-it.ac.jp/assets/iwatukibus.pdf)

For bus stop locations and details, see the [official access guide](https://www.shibaura-it.ac.jp/about/facility/omiya_campus.html#omiya_train) (in Japanese).

## From major stations and airports

- From **Omiya Station**: about 6 min to Higashi-Omiya on the JR Utsunomiya Line
- From **Tokyo Station**: about 50 min to Higashi-Omiya via the JR Ueno-Tokyo Line (through service to the Utsunomiya Line)
- From **Haneda Airport**: Keikyu Line to Shinagawa, then the JR Ueno-Tokyo Line to Higashi-Omiya; about 1.5 hours in total
- From **Narita Airport**: Keisei Skyliner to Nippori, then JR from Ueno on the Utsunomiya Line to Higashi-Omiya; about 1 hour 40 min in total

From Higashi-Omiya Station, take the shuttle bus or walk to the campus.
