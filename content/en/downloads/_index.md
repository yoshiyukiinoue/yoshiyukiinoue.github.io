Numerical data from our papers. Please read each paper for details. If you have any questions or need other data, feel free to contact us (yinoue_at_shibaura-it.ac.jp, replace _at_ with @).

- **Extragalactic Background Light (EBL) model** — [Inoue et al. 2013](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2013ApJ...768..197I)
  - Model data of the EBL intensity (z=0), proper photon density, and gamma-ray opacity
  - Data file: [EBL_data_yinoue.tar.gz](https://yoshiyukiinoue.github.io/page/files/EBL_data_yinoue.tar.gz)
- **A bound on the primordial black hole abundance** — [Inoue & Kusenko 2017](https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.00791)
  - Upper bound on the PBH mass fraction based on the luminosity function of X-ray binaries
  - Data file: [Limit_Omega_PBH_IK17.dat](https://yoshiyukiinoue.github.io/page/files/Limit_Omega_PBH_IK17.dat)
- **Extragalactic X-ray Binary Catalog** — [Inoue, Yabe, & Ueda 2021](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021PASJ...73.1315I)
  - [Catalog page](https://yoshiyukiinoue.github.io/page/exrbcatalog/)
